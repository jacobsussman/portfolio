Summer Film School

Founder, Instructor

For three years, I have instructed and managed a two-week summer film school for middle schoolers.

Students will learn how to write screenplays, plan visual storyboards and production outlines, operate professional digital camera gear and edit video in Final Cut Pro X.

Run in collaboration with Zander Cowen.
