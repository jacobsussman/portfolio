Five Miles Astride the River

Director, Producer, Editor

Five Miles Astride the River: The Story of Wayland is a 17-minute documentary that traces the historical development of Wayland, Massachusetts, a small town located 20 miles west of Boston. The film was funded in 2013 by the Wayland Historical Society in honor of the town’s 375th anniversary.

The film explores three eras of Wayland's development: its origins in the colonial era, its rapid development during the Industrial Revolution and its transition to a modern suburb after World War II.

Co-Directed and Co-Produced with Zander Cowen.
