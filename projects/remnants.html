Remnants of the American War

Director, UX Designer, Front-End Developer

I am currently in production on an interactive documentary exploring the legacy of the American War in Vietnam.

The project is a multimedia mixing of photographic portraiture and documentary film, weaving together the stories of veterans, social workers, and artists whom I encountered during a month long journey from Hanoi to Ho Chi Minh City.

The website was built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery, and is mobile-responsive. It will be released during the summer of 2016.
