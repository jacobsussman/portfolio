Parque

Director, Producer, Editor

I am currently in production on a feature documentary film exploring the lives of 20 volunteers at Parque Ambue Ari, a wildlife refuge in the Bolivian jungle.

Crafted in a candid, observational style, the film transports viewer to a fantastic world where backpackers care for pumas, jaguars, and monkeys.

The film will be released during the fall of 2016.

Co-Directed and Co-Produced with Zander Cowen.
