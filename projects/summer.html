Summer Film School

Founder, Instructor

The Pelham Island Pictures Summer Film School is a two-week summer camp for middle schoolers I have managed and instructed for three years.

Held in Wayland, MA, the camp teaches students how to write screenplays, operate professional film cameras, and edit video in Final Cut Pro X. We have run five full sessions, incurring a waitlist each year to rave reviews from our community.
